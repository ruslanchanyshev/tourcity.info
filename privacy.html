Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

1. Information Collection

TourCity is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require account registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers from our app users.

2. Location Data

Our app requires access to your device's location to show your position on the map and provide navigation. This location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Offline Usage

The core functionality of TourCity works offline. Data updates are downloaded from our servers, but no user-specific information is transmitted during this process.

4. Third-Party Services

We use MapLibre for map rendering. MapLibre may process basic technical data for rendering purposes, but it is not linked to your identity.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tourcity.info
